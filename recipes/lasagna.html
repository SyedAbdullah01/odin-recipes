Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with a rich combination of meat, cheese, and sauce. To make it, start by cooking lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. In a separate pan, prepare a meat sauce by browning ground beef or sausage, then adding garlic, onions, tomato sauce, and seasoning with salt, pepper, and herbs like basil and oregano. For the cheese filling, mix ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and Parmesan. To assemble the lasagna, layer the noodles in a baking dish, followed by the meat sauce, cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat these layers until the dish is full, finishing with a layer of mozzarella on top. Bake in a preheated oven at 375째F (190째C) for about 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving for the perfect, hearty meal!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C). Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. ook the ground beef or sausage in a skillet until browned, then add tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, then remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until bubbly.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes, then serve and enjoy!
