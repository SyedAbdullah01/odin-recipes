French Fries

Description:

French fries are a classic, crispy, and golden snack loved by many around the world. Made from fresh potatoes, theyâ€™re sliced into thin strips and deep-fried until they achieve a perfect balance of a crunchy exterior and a soft, fluffy interior. The rich, salty flavor pairs wonderfully with a variety of dipping sauces, from ketchup to mayo. Whether enjoyed as a side dish with a burger or as a standalone treat, French fries offer a satisfying, savory experience that is both simple and irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Oil

Recipe:

  1. Cut potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or fryer.
  3. Fry potatoes until golden, then drain and season with salt.
